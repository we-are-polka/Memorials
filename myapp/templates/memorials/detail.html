Obituary

{{ memorial.name }}, 68, of Springfield, passed away peacefully on November 22, 2023 surrounded by his loving family. John was born on January 15, 1955 to Robert and Margaret Williams.

He is survived by his beloved wife of 42 years, Susan; his children Michael and Emily; and his grandchildren Olivia and James. John was a dedicated family man who always put others before himself.

An accomplished engineer, John worked at Springfield Manufacturing for 35 years where he was respected by colleagues and friends alike. In his free time, he enjoyed fishing, woodworking, and spending time with his grandchildren.
